Fender

Secure AWS Based File Sharing

Welcome to Fender, a demonstration project showcasing a simple and secure cloud-based file sharing system. Easily share your files and folders, or receive them from others. This service is intended for educational and portfolio purposes.

How Fender Works:

  • To share, click "Share File" or "Share Folder" and upload.
  • You will receive a unique link/ID to share.
  • To receive, click "Receive File" or "Receive Folder" and enter the link/ID.
  • Files are securely stored and transferred via AWS S3.
