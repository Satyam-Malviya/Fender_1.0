Fender

AWS Based File Sharing System

How Fender Works:

  • To share a file, click on "Share a File" and upload your document.
  • You will receive a unique link to share with others.
  • To receive a file, click "Receive a File" and enter the link.
  • Files are securely stored and transferred via AWS S3.
